O'Gara Motorsport

A Club within a club

O’Gara Motorsports offers its clients a supreme, luxury motorsports lifestyle experience at Thermal Club, Palm Springs, with a highly knowledgeable team of experts including former pro race car driver, Parris Mullins. O’Gara Motorsports helps clients select the right race car based on their individual goals and provides best-in-class retrofitting and customization for ultimate track performance.

Clients can expect trackside vehicle delivery, personalized coaching and handling of the vehicle, and exclusive access to the O’Gara facility and private track days.The O'Gara experience sets the bar higher than any other dealer group due to our innovative thinking and drive to push the entire industry forward. We don't simply replicate the highline car buying experience: we reinvent it. Whether we are working with Italian designers to spec fashion-inspired breathtaking automobiles, providing world-class events themed after cultural holidays and unique lifestyles, or using advances in social media and tech to serve you in new and exciting ways, we are constantly redefining what it means to deliver award winning service.
